Modern UI

uBlock Origin features a purely functional UI that simply gets out of your way. Its innovative design allows seamless user interaction.

Light on resources

Memory and CPU usage is far less than alternatives such as Adblock and uBlock.

Philosophy

uBlock Origin (or uBlock₀) is not an ad blocker; it's a general-purpose blocker. uBlock₀ blocks ads through its support of the Adblock Plus filter syntax. uBlock₀ extends the syntax and is designed to work with custom rules and filters.
